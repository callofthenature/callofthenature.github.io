Accommodations

Deluxe Tripple Bed Room

The wooden paned windows offer beautiful scenic sights. The rooms are well furnished with classic imperial furniture that gives you a feel of the olden times.

Tea Estate & Attractions

Heritage Tea Estate

Wander through the century-old sprawling tea plantations that surround the bungalow. Take guided tours of the tea factory to witness traditional tea processing from plucking to tasting.

Colonial Tea Bungalow

Experience life in a 100-year-old British-era tea bungalow featuring classic imperial architecture, vintage furniture, fireplaces, and sweeping verandahs overlooking endless tea gardens.

Gorumara National Park

Located just 20 kilometers away, explore this renowned sanctuary famous for one-horned rhinoceros, Asian elephants, Indian bison, and over 200 bird species through jeep and elephant safaris.

Fafar Kheti Waterfall

Visit this idyllic spot just 30 minutes downhill, featuring a gorgeous waterfall cascading over the Chel River, surrounded by lush greenery and offering spectacular bird watching opportunities.

Lava & Rishyap Hill Stations

Explore nearby mountain villages offering stunning Kanchenjunga views, monasteries, pine forests, and the famous Tiffin Dara viewpoint with its 360-degree panorama of snow-capped peaks.

Neora Valley National Park

Discover pristine forests rich in rhododendrons, oak, bamboo, and ferns. Home to rare species like red pandas, Asiatic black bears, leopards, and numerous Himalayan birds.

Coronation Bridge

Visit this historic British-era bridge spanning the Teesta River, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and mountains. A perfect spot for photography and river picnics.

Suntaleykhola Village

Experience the charm of this alpine village nestled amidst evergreen forests. Known as a butterfly paradise with diverse flora, fauna, and traditional Lepcha culture.

Location

In the Heart of Dooars Tea Country

Gorubathan Tea Heritage Bungalow is majestically perched atop a hill in the Dooars region, approximately 20 kilometers from Malbazar. Nestled within sprawling tea plantations with the Eastern Himalayas forming a spectacular backdrop, this colonial gem offers an unmatched escape into West Bengal's rich tea heritage.

Our strategic hilltop location provides:

  • Panoramic views of endless tea gardens and Himalayan peaks
  • 20 km from Gorumara National Park entrance
  • Prime access to Dooars wildlife circuit
  • Surrounded by pristine forest vegetation
  • Gateway to Lava, Rishyap, and Kalimpong hill stations
