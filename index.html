Our Story

Crafting Extraordinary Experiences in Nature's Embrace

Born from a deep passion for nature and adventure, Call of the Nature was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to connect travelers with the untamed beauty of our natural world through carefully curated experiences that go beyond the ordinary.

We believe that true adventure lies not in crowded tourist destinations, but in the hidden gems where nature reveals its most authentic self. Our handpicked collection of offbeat stays and wildlife safaris offers you the chance to disconnect from the chaos of modern life and reconnect with the rhythms of the wild.

Every experience we craft tells a story - of conservation, of local communities, and of the incredible biodiversity that makes India one of the world's most remarkable wildlife destinations. We are committed to responsible tourism that not only creates unforgettable memories for our guests but also contributes to the preservation of these pristine environments for future generations.

Our Mission

To provide transformative travel experiences that foster a deeper connection with nature while supporting conservation efforts and local communities across India's most spectacular landscapes.
