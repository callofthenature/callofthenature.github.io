Sanjay Dubri Wildlife Safari

📍 Sidhi District, Madhya Pradesh, India

Discover the hidden gem of Madhya Pradesh with exclusive tiger, leopard, and sloth bear encounters in pristine sal forests and grasslands

Discover the Hidden Tiger Paradise

Sanjay Dubri National Park, a lesser-known gem in Madhya Pradesh's Sidhi district, offers an intimate wildlife experience away from crowded tourist circuits. This pristine tiger reserve spans over 1,674 square kilometers of sal forests, bamboo groves, and grasslands, providing sanctuary to diverse wildlife including tigers, leopards, and the rare sloth bears.

Legacy of the White Tiger

This remarkable landscape holds special significance in tiger conservation history - it's where the famous white tiger "Mohan" was discovered in 1951 by the Maharaja of Rewa. Today, most white tigers in zoos worldwide trace their lineage back to this very forest, and one of the park's ranges bears Mohan's name in tribute.

Why Choose Sanjay Dubri?

Experience the raw wilderness of central India with minimal tourist crowds. Our carefully planned safari itinerary focuses on the park's core areas where tigers, leopards, and sloth bears are most active, offering exceptional photography opportunities and authentic wildlife encounters in their natural habitat.

Tiger-Focused Safari Experience

Our specialized 3-day program includes 4 tiger-focused safaris designed for serious wildlife enthusiasts. With strategic positioning in prime territories and expert local guides who understand animal behavior patterns, maximize your chances of witnessing these magnificent predators in action.

Detailed Itinerary

1
Arrival at Sanjay Dubri Resort – Evening Safari
Arrival: Pickup from Beohari Railway Station and check-in at Sanjay Dubri Resort by afternoon.
Lunch: Welcome lunch at the resort with local cuisine.
Evening Safari: First tiger-focused safari during golden hours for optimal wildlife photography.
Night: Dinner and overnight stay at resort with astrophotography opportunities if weather permits.
2
Double Safari Day – Prime Tiger Territory
Morning Safari: Early morning safari targeting prime tiger and leopard territories with expert wildlife tracking.
Breakfast & Rest: Return to resort for breakfast and relaxation during midday heat.
Lunch: Traditional lunch at resort with regional specialties.
Evening Safari: Second evening safari focusing on sloth bear habitats and golden light photography.
Night: Dinner and overnight stay with optional night sky photography session.
3
Final Safari & Departure
Morning Safari: Final tiger-focused safari in core areas for last wildlife encounters and photography.
Breakfast: Leisurely breakfast at resort while sharing safari experiences.
Check Out: Resort check-out and preparation for departure.
Departure: Drop-off at Beohari Railway Station for onward journey.
