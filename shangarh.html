Shangarh Mountain Retreat

📍 Shangarh, Himachal Pradesh

Experience one of the most authentic parts of Himachal in all its glory!

Welcome to Shangarh Mountain Retreat!

Nestled in the serene mountain village of Shangarh, this one of a kind Homestay is a haven for those seeking an escape into nature's embrace. Surrounded by lush greenery and cradled by the mighty Himalayas, this hidden gem in the Himalayan foothills offers a retreat like no other.

The local hospitality adds a warm touch to the experience, inviting travelers to unwind and connect with the natural wonders that grace this enchanting region providing an authentic and unforgettable experience.

Rooms

Double Bedded Room

Comfortable double bed accommodation with mountain views and modern amenities.

First Floor Room

Elevated room with panoramic views of the surrounding meadows and mountains.

Dormitory

Budget-friendly shared accommodation perfect for backpackers and solo travelers.

Nearby Attractions

Shangarh Meadows

A visit to Shangarh meadows will leave you breathless: they are large, lush green, calm, and beautiful.

Barshangarh Waterfall

Reached by a short drive and then a hike of half a kilometre, the Barshangarh Falls are a pleasant delight on this mountainous terrain.

Raila Fort & Waterfall

Drive down for a short tour, covering the twin towers, the village of Raila, the Raila Temple and the hidden waterfalls.

Thini Thatch & Pundrik Lake

The Great Himalayan National Park offers many mesmerizing trekking trails for you to traverse.

Explore Nearby Villages

Plan a visit to the nearby villages, surrounded by apple orchards and the famous wooden Himachali houses.

Bird Watching at GHNP

Shangarh is home to a variety of native birds that welcome travelers. GHNP serves as a habitat for nearly 100 different bird species.

Travel to Tirthan Valley

The magnificent Tirthan Valley is only a two-hour drive from Shangarh and well worth a visit. Gushaini and Sarchi are breathtakingly beautiful places.

Jalori Pass & Jibhi

Plan a trek to Raghupur Fort & Sereolsar Lake. This trek will take you through the dramatic high altitude landscapes. Also explore the lush green landscapes and waterfalls at Jibhi.

Location

Nestled in the Heart of Shangarh

Suhaana Ghar is strategically located in Village Goshti, Shangarh, offering easy access to the famous Shangarh meadows and other natural attractions. The homestay provides a perfect base for exploring the Great Himalayan National Park and the surrounding valleys.

Our location offers:

  • Direct access to Shangarh meadows
  • Panoramic mountain views
  • Peaceful, pollution-free environment
  • Traditional Himachali village experience

How to Reach

✈️

By Air

The closest airport to Shangarh is the Bhuntar Airport at approximately 47 km. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.

🚂

By Train

The closest railway station is Chandigarh Railway Station. It is approximately 250 km away. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.

🚌

By Bus

Firstly, you will have to reach Aut for which you can take any bus going towards Kullu, Kasol and Manali. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.

🚗

By Car

If you are taking your vehicle or hiring a cab, there are two options. You can either choose the way via Shimla or Kiratpur.
